# Paper Overview: LPFQA: Long-Tail Professional Forum-based QA

## Problem
Mainstream LLMs excel at common knowledge but degrade significantly when faced with **long-tail professional queries**—niche, low-frequency technical problems found in specialized forums (e.g., Electrical Engineering, Life Sciences).

## Method
Constructs a benchmark from authentic professional forums across 20 academic and industrial fields. It uses **hierarchical difficulty modeling** and realistic "user personas" to test if a model can provide precise answers to practical, real-world problems.

## Benchmarks / Datasets
* **GPQA Comparison:** Models score high on GPQA but drop to **45%–55%** on LPFQA.
* **GPT-5 Lead:** GPT-5 (High) achieves ~54.4%, still failing to reach a 60% passing threshold.

## FoxBrain Relevance
LPFQA is critical for our **Technical Support Bot**. It proves that we cannot rely on base model "intelligence" alone for niche engineering problems; we must prioritize high-quality, forum-derived RAG to bridge the long-tail knowledge gap.
