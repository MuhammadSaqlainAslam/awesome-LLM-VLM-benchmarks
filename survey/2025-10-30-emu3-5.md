# Emu3.5: Native Multimodal Models are World Learners (2025)

## Problem
Prior unified multimodal models suffered from two critical limitations: sequential token-by-token image generation was prohibitively slow for practical use, and models lacked robust world-modeling capacity — the ability to learn visual and physical dynamics from internet-scale video. Emu3.5 tackles both simultaneously, training a single native model on video-centric internet data to internalize general visual dynamics and introducing a fast inference method to make image generation practical at scale.

## Method
Emu3.5 is a 34.1B-parameter unified model (31.2B transformer + 2.9B embedding, vocabulary of 282,926 tokens) trained on 13+ trillion multimodal tokens drawn predominantly from sequential internet video frames and transcripts. The key architectural innovation is **Discrete Diffusion Adaptation (DiDA)**, which converts standard sequential token decoding into bidirectional parallel prediction — achieving approximately 20× speedup in per-image generation inference without quality loss. Large-scale reinforcement learning post-training further enhances multimodal reasoning and generation. Emu3.5 supports native interleaved vision-language input/output ("X2I" any-to-image generation), enabling flexible chained image–text–image reasoning.

## Benchmarks / Datasets
- **TIIF-Bench:** Text-to-image instruction following
- **OneIG-EN:** General image generation quality
- **LeX-Bench:** English text rendering quality in generated images
- **LongText-Bench:** Long-form text rendering in images (EN + ZH)
- **CVTG-2K:** Complex visual text generation
- **Interleaved generation task suites** comparing against GPT-Image-1, Gemini 2.5 Flash Image, Qwen-Image, and FLUX.1

## Key Results
- **TIIF-Bench:** 94.03 overall (FLUX.1 [dev]: 70.72; Qwen-Image: 90.30)
- **OneIG-EN:** 0.564 overall (Gemini 2.5 Flash Image: 0.550; Qwen-Image: 0.539)
- **LeX-Bench:** Easy-recall 0.98, Medium 0.96, Hard 0.87 — surpasses GPT-Image-1 and Gemini 2.5 Flash Image
- **LongText-Bench:** 0.952 avg (EN: 0.976, ZH: 0.928) vs Qwen-Image 0.944
- **X2I win rate:** ~51–65% against Gemini 2.5 Flash Image across interleaved generation tasks
- **DiDA speedup:** ~20× inference acceleration with no measurable quality regression

## FoxBrain Relevance
Emu3.5's video-driven world-learning paradigm is directly applicable to Foxconn's factory floor, where sequential visual data (inspection camera feeds, assembly line video) encodes rich physical and procedural dynamics. The DiDA fast inference technique could enable real-time visual generation and annotation in production environments. The model's strength in text-rich image generation (circuit diagrams, engineering schematics with dense text) is particularly relevant for FoxBrain's manufacturing document understanding tasks.
