## Problem
The original MMMU benchmark has become "saturated," with top models scoring near-human levels, making it difficult to distinguish true "Pro" level multimodal reasoning.

## Method
**MMMU-Pro** raises the ceiling by introducing 11,500 expert-level questions that require university-level knowledge across Science, Engineering, and Tech, with much more complex visual inputs.

## Benchmarks / Datasets
- **Benchmark:** MMMU-Pro
- **Dataset:** 11.5K Expert-level Multimodal Questions
- **Metrics:** Zero-shot Accuracy, Multi-step Visual Reasoning

## Key Results
While models scored 80%+ on MMMU, the same models only average **46.2% on MMMU-Pro**, proving that professional-level visual reasoning is still a major frontier.

## FoxBrain Relevance
This is our "North Star" for multimodal capability. We should track FoxBrain's progress against the MMMU-Pro leaderboard to verify we are moving toward "Expert AI" status.
