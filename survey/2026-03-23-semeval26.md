## Problem
Current multilingual benchmarks often focus on translation rather than the nuances of logical reasoning across diverse linguistic and cultural logic structures.

## Method
**SemEval-2026 Task 11** focuses on Multilingual Logical Reasoning, using syllogisms and logic puzzles translated and adapted into 20+ languages to test core reasoning independent of language.

## Benchmarks / Datasets
- **Benchmark:** SemEval-26 Task 11
- **Dataset:** Multilingual Logic Suite (20 languages)
- **Metrics:** Logical Accuracy, Cross-lingual Consistency

## Key Results
The "Reasoning Gap" between English and low-resource languages (e.g., Swahili, Vietnamese) remains high, with a **22% difference** in logical accuracy despite perfect translation.

## FoxBrain Relevance
If FoxBrain is marketed globally, we must ensure logical consistency isn't language-dependent. This benchmark helps us evaluate our model's performance in non-English markets.
