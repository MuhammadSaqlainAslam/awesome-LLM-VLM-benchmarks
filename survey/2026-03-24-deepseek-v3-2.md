# Paper Overview: DeepSeek-V3.2: Pushing Open Reasoning Boundaries

## Problem
Open-weights models often struggle with high-order logical consistency during tool use. V3.2 focuses on **"Thinking Mode"**—a dual-mode system that plans steps internally before taking action.

## Method
Introduces **DeepSeek Sparse Attention (DSA)** to reduce compute costs by 50%+. It utilizes a **Scalable Reinforcement Learning (RL)** framework that aligns the model against "Gold Medal" performance in the 2025 International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO).

## Benchmarks / Datasets
* **IMO-ProofBench:** Surpasses GPT-5 and Gemini 3.0 Pro in verified proof generation.
* **MMLU-Pro:** **78.4%**.
* **SWE-bench Verified:** **42.1%** (Strongest open-weights coding model).

## FoxBrain Relevance
DeepSeek-V3.2 is our **Open-Source fallback** for high-reasoning tasks. Its reasoning variant should be used for complex algorithmic verification when privacy prevents using Claude or GPT-5.4.
