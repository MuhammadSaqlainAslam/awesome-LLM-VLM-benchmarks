# OlymMATH: Challenging the Boundaries of Reasoning with Olympiad-Level Math (2025)

## Problem
Frontier models are rapidly saturating existing math benchmarks like AIME and MATH. There was no bilingual (English + Chinese) Olympiad-level benchmark with fully verifiable numerical answers that was demonstrably clean of web contamination, and no benchmark that specifically isolated a "harder than AIME" difficulty tier to stress-test reasoning models beyond existing ceilings.

## Method
200 problems were manually sourced from **printed publications only** (not online repositories) specifically to prevent contamination. Every problem was independently verified by at least two domain experts, including a **China Mathematical Olympiad silver medalist** and two provincial first-prize winners. English translations were produced iteratively with LLM assistance and expert validation. Problems span four mathematical domains and two verified difficulty tiers: **EASY** (comparable to AIME) and **HARD** (harder than AIME). All problems have precise numerical answers enabling rule-based automated evaluation — no judge LLM required. Both Chinese (ZH) and English (EN) versions are released. From Renmin University of China and BAAI.

## Benchmarks / Datasets
- **200 problems** across 4 domains and 2 difficulty tiers (EASY / HARD), in both EN and ZH
- **Domains:** Algebra (inequalities, sequences, trigonometry), Geometry (solid + analytic), Number Theory (divisibility, Diophantine equations), Combinatorics (graph theory, permutations)
- Contamination prevention: sourced exclusively from printed materials not indexed online
- Expert verification: CMO silver medalist + provincial prize winners

## Key Results (Pass@1)

| Model | EN-EASY | EN-HARD | ZH-EASY | ZH-HARD |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| DeepSeek-R1 | 79.6% | 19.5% | 70.4% | 15.9% |
| o3-mini (high) | 91.4% | 31.2% | 90.5% | 32.9% |
| **Gemini 2.5 Pro Exp** | **92.2%** | **58.4%** | **90.8%** | **55.4%** |

Even the best model (Gemini 2.5 Pro) scores only **58.4% on the hardest English subset** and 55.4% on Chinese — with a clear language performance gap across all models. The EASY tier (AIME-level) is increasingly saturated; the HARD tier remains the relevant frontier.

## FoxBrain Relevance
OlymMATH's bilingual EN/ZH structure is directly relevant to FoxBrain's deployment context, where both English and Mandarin technical documentation must be processed with equal rigour. The contamination-free sourcing methodology (printed materials only) is a template for constructing FoxBrain's internal manufacturing and engineering math evaluation sets — ensuring test integrity when benchmarking against proprietary Foxconn process data. The EASY/HARD tiering provides a practical scaffold for progressively gating FoxBrain's engineering calculation capabilities.
