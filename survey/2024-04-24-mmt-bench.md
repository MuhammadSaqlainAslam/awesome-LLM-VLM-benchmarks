# MMT-Bench: A Comprehensive Multimodal Benchmark for Evaluating LVLMs Towards Multitask AGI (2024)

## Problem
Existing multimodal evaluation benchmarks cover a limited number of tasks and test only rudimentary visual capabilities, making them inadequate for tracking LVLM development towards genuine multi-task AGI. No benchmark simultaneously assessed the breadth of expert-level visual recognition, localization, reasoning, and planning skills required across real-world multimodal scenarios such as autonomous driving and embodied navigation.

## Method
MMT-Bench systematically curates **31,325 multi-choice visual questions** from diverse multimodal scenarios — including vehicle driving, embodied navigation, medical imaging, and document understanding — and organises them into a hierarchical taxonomy of **32 core meta-tasks** and **162 subtasks**. Each question requires expert knowledge and deliberate visual reasoning rather than simple pattern matching. A "task map" is introduced to discover in-domain and out-of-domain task relationships across LVLMs. 20+ LVLMs were evaluated, including proprietary models (GPT-4V, GeminiProVision) and leading open-source models (InternVL-Chat). Published at **ICML 2024** by **OpenGVLab** (Shanghai AI Lab, SenseTime, CUHK, SJTU; arXiv: 2404.16006).

## Benchmarks / Datasets
- **31,325 multi-choice visual questions**
- **32 core meta-tasks**, including: Visual Recognition, Localization, OCR, Counting, High-Level Naming, Image Retrieval, 3D Understanding, Visual Comparison, Visual Grounding, Document Understanding, Attribute Recognition, Scene Planning, Image-to-Image Translation, Relationship Recognition, Image Quality Assessment, Emotion Recognition, Medical Image Understanding, Visual Commonsense Reasoning, Geometric Naming, and more
- **162 subtasks** spanning fine-grained specialisations within each meta-task
- Multimodal scenarios: vehicle driving, embodied navigation, medical imaging, document understanding, general visual QA

## Key Results

| Model | Overall Accuracy |
|---|---|
| **GPT-4o** | **Leading (exact score not published at release)** |
| InternVL-Chat-v1.2-34B | 63.4% |
| Qwen-VL-Plus | 62.3% |
| GPT-4V | 62.0% |
| GeminiProVision | 61.6% |

- **GPT-4o** surpasses all other models at evaluation time, particularly excelling on visual recognition, captioning, and visual perception tasks
- All models struggle with fine-grained visual cognition and spatial understanding — **perception error is the most common failure mode**
- The task map reveals clear capability clusters: models strong on visual recognition tend to transfer well to localization but not to document understanding

## FoxBrain Relevance
MMT-Bench's 32 meta-task taxonomy maps directly onto Foxconn's diverse factory-floor visual AI requirements: OCR (reading component labels, schematics), Counting (inventory, defect density), Medical Image Understanding (adapted to industrial inspection), Document Understanding (technical manuals, compliance forms), and 3D Understanding (spatial assembly verification). The task map methodology enables FoxBrain's evaluation to identify specific bottleneck meta-tasks without running all 31K questions — enabling targeted fine-tuning on the exact visual subtasks where FoxBrain underperforms for Foxconn's deployment scenarios.
