# Paper Overview: GPT-5.4 mini and nano: Efficiency at Scale

## Problem
High-volume enterprise workloads (classification, UI navigation, sub-agents) are too expensive on flagship GPT-5.4 models. These "mini" variants aim to provide "Computer Use" capabilities at commodity pricing.

## Method
Distilled versions of the GPT-5.4 architecture. The **mini** variant includes native **Computer Use** support, allowing it to interpret screenshots and click UI elements with nearly the same precision as the flagship, while running 2x faster than GPT-5 mini.

## Benchmarks / Datasets
* **OSWorld-Verified:** **72.1%** (Approaching the 75.0% of the GPT-5.4 flagship).
* **SWE-bench Pro:** **54.4%** (Extremely high for a small model).
* **GPQA Diamond:** **88.0%**.

## FoxBrain Relevance
We should replace GPT-4o-mini with **GPT-5.4 mini** for all UI-automation sub-agents. It provides a massive upgrade in "Screenshot-to-Action" reliability.
